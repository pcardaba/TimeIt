set_canvas_scale  scale_factor
                  [-help]


Sets the horizontal (time axis) scale of the waveform canvas: how much canvas
space a time unit takes. This is the same zoom the Shift+Mouse Wheel performs,
and it is what a saved diagram uses to restore the zoom it was saved with.
The vertical direction is never scaled.

  scale_factor : Mandatory. Positive number. A larger value expands the time
                 axis (zoom in), a smaller one compresses it (zoom out).


Examples:


1.
   set_canvas_scale 2.5

Expands the time axis: the waveforms are drawn 2.5 times wider.


2.
   set_canvas_scale 0.1

Compresses the time axis, to fit a long diagram in the window.
