redraw  [-help]


Redraws all canvas drawings: every signal, timing marker, split and
annotation is repainted on both the visible waveform canvas and the
hidden high-resolution canvas. The command takes no arguments.

Normally the application redraws automatically after each change, so
this command is only needed to force a refresh manually (for example
from a script, or after changing several timing variables with plain
Tcl "set" commands).


Examples:


1.
   redraw

Refreshes the waveform canvas.
